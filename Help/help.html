Rett Syndrome Portal is a free web server that offers a wide platform for the analysis and sharing of data related to Rett syndrome. This portal was created to provide centralized access to transcriptomic data from subjects with Rett syndrome, which has been collected from various human and mouse-based studies.

The access to Rett Synrome Portal is public and free.
